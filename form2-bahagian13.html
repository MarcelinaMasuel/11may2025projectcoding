FORM 2 - 𝛣𝐴𝐻𝐴𝐺𝐼𝐴𝑁 13

Kalau kau nak tau, aku banyak kali mention nama kau in my prayers. Every morning and every night. I still remember how I pray for you while I'm crying... I asked God to make your life easier. I asked God to tell you how much I love and care about you. And, it's the first time I asked God to make someone's life easier and tell them how much I love and care about them. Even tho I'm scared to mention a friend's name that I love, sebab setiap kali aku mention nama sorang kawan, dia selalu tinggalkan aku. But, I'll take the risk, for you.
