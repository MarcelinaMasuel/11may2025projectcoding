FORM 2 - BAHAGIAN 16

Do you still remember when I wasn’t chosen for basketball? I was so freaking sad. Teacher said, "Sebenarnya saya mahu pilih si kecik (aku) tu." "Kamu boleh, bagus kamu bawa bola, cantik. Tapi, passing kamu tak bagus." "Kalau passing tak bagus, nanti dalam game, habis." I burst into tears. Friends comforted me and tried to persuade the teacher to let me join basketball, but the teacher gave no response. I walked out with tears running down my face. "Aku taknak balik dulu, taknak parents aku nampak aku nangis. Aku rehat lah kejap," I said. "Did I not try hard enough? I pushed myself sampai vision blurred, hearing blocked. I ALMOST F*CKING FAINTED IN TRAINING. But all I got was disappointment." Then korang PBSM keluar. You called my name, I turned but looked away. Assuming you didn't see my eyes. Then in the corner of my eyes I saw you walking toward me. You sat beside me and try to comfort me. You were the third person who ever try to comfort me after my mom and Grecia. When you said, "Aw, it’s okay," ARGGHH, MY TEARS FLOWED HARDER! You were the first to let me cry on your shoulder. I wanted you to stay longer but I forced myself to stand, taknak kau balik lambat sebab aku. Aku balik with tears still flowing, mind repeating cikgu’s words, but I remembered you wiping my tears. BTW you're the first person who ever wiped my tears. You also the second person who called Grecia when I cried. I’m so grateful for a friend like you. Dari semua kawan aku di Sarawak, you’re the best, my favourite. Maybe moving to Sarawak isn’t that bad because I met you there.
