FORM 2 - 𝛣𝐴𝐻𝐴𝐺𝐼𝐴𝑁 6

Kau ingat lagi tak mainan pistol aku yang aku terjumpa kat sekolah? Yang warna hijau tu. Aku jumpa pistol tu kat koridor astu aku tendang lah tapi bila aku tengok lebih dekat, nampak macam pistol, then aku ambik. I know it's not that important but just want to tell you :). Then time rehat aku show kau, then kau ckp 'tu bukan pistol lah' walaupun orang lain cakap tu pistol but whatever. Then kau ada cakap 'kau ni macam budak² lah'. It's been a long time since someone said that to me. Then my favourite part, time balik kau tanya aku 'mana pistol kau tu?' then aku kasih tunjuk pistol aku. For a moment, I goes silent thinking about the sentence you just said to me. It feels like you're my mom and I'm your child. Bukan apa, sejak aku pindah Sarawak, iaitu 2020, takde siapa pernah tanya pasal mainan aku. Mereka tanya pasal buku aku, alatan tulis aku, botol aku, cermin mata aku, bende bukan mainan lah. You're the first person who said that to me after 5 years.
