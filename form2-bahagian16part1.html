FORM 2 - 𝛣𝐴𝐻𝐴𝐺𝐼𝐴𝑁 16 (𝑃𝐴𝑅𝑇 1)

Do you still remember when I wasn’t chosen for basketball? I was so freaking sad at that time. I thought I did well enough, but no. I still remember the moment when the teacher said, "Sebenarnya saya mahu pilih si kecik (aku) tu." "Kamu boleh, bagus kamu bawa bola, cantik. Tapi, passing kamu tak bagus." "Kalau passing tak bagus, nanti dalam game, habis." I could feel my eyes getting watery. Then I burst into tears. Everyone noticed, and my friends came to comfort me and tried to persuade the teacher to let me join basketball, but the teacher gave no response. Then the teacher told everyone to go home except for the 13 people he had chosen. I walked out of school with tears running down my face. "Aku taknak balik dulu, taknak parents aku nampak aku nangis. Aku rehat lah kejap," I said to myself as I sat on one of the benches, thinking about the things my teacher said to me, over and over again.
