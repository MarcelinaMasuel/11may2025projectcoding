FORM 2 - 𝛣𝐴𝐻𝐴𝐺𝐼𝐴𝑁 16 (𝑃𝐴𝑅𝑇 1)

Do you still remember when aku tak kene pilih bola keranjang? I was so freaking sad that time. I thought I did good enough but, no. The moment when the teacher say, "Sebenarnya saya mahu pilih si kecik (aku) tu." "Kamu boleh, bagus kamu bawa bola, cantik. Tapi, passing kamu tak bagus." "Kalau passing tak bagus, nanti dalam game, habis," I can feel my eyes became watery. Then I bursted into tears. Everyone noticed and my friends came to me, trying to comfort me and pujuk cikgu untuk ambik aku masuk basketball, but the teacher gave no response. Then cikgu suruh balik kecuali 13 orang yang cikgu pilih. I walked out of school with tears running down my face. "Aku taknak balik dulu, taknak parents aku nampak aku nangis. Aku rehat lah kejap," I said to myself as I sat on one of the benches, thinking about those sentences my teacher said to me over and over again.
